As newbie to my major, I'm navigating my way through the creative landscape with an unwavering passion for art, drawing, and video games. Being new to the ICAM visual arts major, every day brings fresh insights and challenges that drive my artistic growth. I'm eager to blend my love for visual arts with the technical aspects of game design, creating immersive experiences that captivate audiences. Whether I'm sketching out new ideas on paper or immersed in the intricate world of video games, I'm constantly seeking ways to push the boundaries of my creativity. Join me on this artistic journey as I bring my unique perspective to life through the fusion of visual arts, game design, and graphic design.
